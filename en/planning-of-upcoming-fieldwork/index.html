Planning of upcoming fieldwork

On April 11th 2024, Fraunhofer Portugal AICOS hosted a team of researchers from the Institute of Philosophy (FLUP) at its headquarters in Porto. The meeting focused on planning the next stage of fieldwork for Project Signo.

The teams are preparing for the start of fieldwork in the coming months, aiming to combine technological development with philosophical inquiry to better shed a light on how human values are embedded into design artefacts, and its implications for techonology for health.
